Multiply Sense Privacy Policy

Multiply Sense is completely offline, no information is sent to any third-party or the creator with an exception of Apple Inc. If you choose to enable voice input in the Quiz Mode of Multiple Sense, the application will access your microphone input and use Apple's voice recongnition technology to process your voice input. Microphone input is accessed solely for this purpose.
